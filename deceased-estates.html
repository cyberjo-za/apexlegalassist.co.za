The Weight of Administrative Burden

When someone passes away, families and executors face not only emotional challenges, but a complex web of administrative tasks. From managing assets to notifying government agencies, settling debts to distributing estates—the process is time-consuming, detail-intensive, and overwhelming.

That's where Apex comes in. We handle the administrative winding-up, allowing you to focus on grieving, supporting your family, and remembering what truly matters.

The Estate Administration Process

We break down the complex estate process into manageable steps, managing each with care and expertise.

1

Initial Assessment

We understand the estate's scope, identify all assets, liabilities, and stakeholders, and develop an administration timeline.

2

Documentation & Filing

We compile necessary documents, prepare required filings with the Master's Office, and notify relevant government agencies and creditors.

3

Asset Management

We coordinate asset valuation, manage property matters, settle debts and taxes, and prepare for distribution.

4

Distribution & Closure

We calculate distributions, coordinate final transfers, communicate with beneficiaries, and prepare final accounting and estate closure.
